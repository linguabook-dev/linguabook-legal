Privacy Policy

LinguaBook · Last updated: July 2026

LinguaBook respects your privacy. The app is designed to work without collecting personal data that identifies you.

1. Data stored on your device

Your imported books, reading progress and settings (language, font, theme, tutorial state) are saved only on your device. We do not operate a server and we receive none of this data. Since there is no account and no server-side storage, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of this data.

2. Translation

When you select a word or sentence to translate, that text is sent to the Google Translate service to obtain the translation. No data that identifies you is attached to the request. Use of this service is governed by Google's privacy policy.

3. Suggestions and bug reports

If you contact us through the in-app feedback form, the content of your message is transmitted through the Formspree service so that it reaches us. Please do not include sensitive personal information in your message.

4. No ads, no tracking

The app shows no advertising and uses no analytics or tracking tools.

5. Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data about children.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any significant change will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us through the contact form available in the LinguaBook app (Settings → Send feedback).
